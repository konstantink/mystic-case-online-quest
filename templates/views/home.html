Intro

We invite you to take part in an online detective game 🕵

You will find 7 easy and exciting tasks that you can complete alone, but for more fun, we recommend playing with two or three people.

Here are some props that will help you to complete the game:

  • a pen,
  • a sheet of paper for notes,
  • a small square piece of paper (even plain note paper or a napkin will do),
  • a mirror (pocket or wall, doesn't matter) or any other reflective surface.

There are hints on each level in case you're stuck.

If everything is clear - enter the word “KEY” in the input field and start the game, Mystic case!
